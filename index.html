PRECAUTIONS TO AVOID COVID-19


DO's


Always wear a face mask outside of home



Wash your hands regularly

Close mouth with elbow while coughing

Sanitize hands regularly while in public

Maintain social distance while in public


Get vaccinated. It's completely SAFE




DONT's



Don't touch eyes with bare hands

Don't gather for ceremonies

Don't travel unless it's manditory

Don't shake hands with others

Don't go out if not feeling well

Don't share your mask or belongings
